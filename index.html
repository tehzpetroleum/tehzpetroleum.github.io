About us

Tehz Petroleum is a privately held energy company with a focus on exploration, acquisition, and development in the continental United States.

As a company with over 75 years of collective experience in the oil and gas industry, we pride ourselves on our relationships with industry veterans and landowners alike. If you're looking for a joint venture partner, or have minerals, leasehold, or working interests you're looking to sell, or have received an offer, or would like us to evaluate your property, don't hesitate to contact us.

Tehz is currently active in Oklahoma and Texas. We are always looking to expand and diversify!
